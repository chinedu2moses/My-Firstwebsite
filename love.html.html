ABOUT LINKSOFT


    Andrew Powrie is the principal of LINKSOFT. Andrew has a Bachelor of Applied Science majoring in Computing from the Queensland University of Technology. This degree was obtained in 1986. Prior to starting his own business Andrew worked in the Queensland Health Department as Software Engineer. Expert in GPRS, GPS, 1XRTT and radio technology, Andrew also possesses extensive IT experience including but not limited to Microsoft Server and desktop operating systems and applications. Andrew takes an active role in all aspects of our Software Development to ensure that the LINKSOFT range of software applications continues to be a market leader in Data Dispatching for the Limousine Industry. Ron Wheeler oversees our administration support and marketing functions. Prior to joining us, Ron held senior positions in the emergency services industries where he was awarded a commendation and a national medal. His flair for procedural organisation is reflected in LINKSOFT’s attention to detail and administrative procedures. As a testament to Ron’s hands on experience with both Software and Hardware and his ability to work closely with all members of the LINKSOFT Team along with his exceptional interpersonal skills, Ron now adds LINKSOFT Software Support to his many talents.
